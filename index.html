About Me

Seirra Dawson is a HR Analyst for Hearst Television in Charlotte, NC. After receiving a B.A. in Business Management, she went into the US Army and commissioned as an officer.

Prior to moving to Charlotte, she worked in the Middle East and Africa as a government contractor. In her free time, she enjoys visiting countries abroad. She has visited 4 out of the 7 continents. She also enjoys the UNCC Coding boot camp course.
